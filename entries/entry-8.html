Louis Vuitton (PARIS:MC.PA +0.38%) has revealed its new Fall 2024 menswear collection, which harbors a key focus on everyday adaptability. As the seasons begin to change, the French Maison understands the intermediary collection's need to be versatile, functional and appealing. Specifically, the collection looks at how adaptable dress codes change over time. With it, the house posits an expanded vision of contemporary work-to-play uniforms which expands the cadence of everyday dressing.

The Design

Designed by Louis Vuitton's Studio Prêt-à-Porte Homme, the collection presents a hybridized take on casual and professional dress notes. In the pursuit of honoring transition, the collection looks to mix up style, whether it be through the communication between fabrics or the play of layering. Boldly hued suiting appears with textured fabric and LV detailing and is paired with calmer elements like a crewneck sweater and chunky tennis shoes.

Availability

The Fall 2024 menswear collection from Louis Vuitton will be available at select boutiques worldwide starting in October. Fashion enthusiasts can expect a diverse range of styles and choices that seamlessly blend sophistication and comfort for the modern man.
