G SHOCK X LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

The Collaboration Between The Watch Company and the Game.

About

We begin with the rugged GA-110 and bring the popular and charismatic League of Legends champion, Jinx, straight into the action. Boldly printed in vivid color, the bezel and band evoke the wild maverick and her signature weapon, the Super Megadeath rocket. This playful motif is echoed in the small hand shaped like Jinx's rocket on the inset dial at the 9 o'clock position.

More

This collaboration model is packed with League of Legends energy, including the game logo on the case back and band loop and special packaging designed with League fans in mind.

The Game

Released in 2009, League of Legends is Riot Games' debut title and one of the most-played competitive games in the world, engaging millions of players daily in 20+ officially-supported languages. A mainstay of the MOBA genre, in League, two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy each other's base in a game that blends the speed, strategy, and intensity of an RTS with immersive RPG elements.

Availability

The watch is publicly available at all retail G-Shock stores.

THE INTRICATE DESIGN

Take a look at some of the watches features.

The G-Shock x League of Legends watch boasts a design inspired by the game's immersive universe. From the bezel to the strap, every detail reflects the iconic elements of League of Legends, capturing the essence of champions and their epic battles. Built on the foundation of G-Shock's legendary toughness, this collaboration watch is shock-resistant and water-resistant, ensuring it can withstand the most intense gaming sessions or real-world adventures. Its robust construction aligns with the game's spirit of endurance and resilience.
