PRADA X ADIDAS BY JERRY LORENZO

The Collaboration Between The Watch Company and the Game.

About

Introducing the groundbreaking collaboration between two titans of fashion and sportswear – Prada and Adidas, curated by the renowned designer Jerry Lorenzo. This exclusive Prada x Adidas x Jerry Lorenzo collaboration redefines the intersection of luxury, athleticism, and contemporary street style, delivering a collection that seamlessly blends high-end craftsmanship with the urban edge that defines modern fashion.

More

The Prada x Adidas Jerry Lorenzo collaboration showcases an elevated take on sportswear, combining Prada's luxury sensibilities with Jerry Lorenzo's distinct urban style. The result is a collection that effortlessly transitions from the streets to high-end fashion settings.

The Design

Jerry Lorenzo's design ethos breathes new life into iconic Adidas silhouettes, creating a harmonious blend of performance and fashion. Each piece is carefully reimagined with premium materials, distinctive detailing, and a nod to both brands' heritage.

Availability

Expect nothing less than the finest craftsmanship and materials from this collaboration. Prada's legacy of excellence meets Adidas' commitment to innovation, resulting in apparel and footwear that not only looks exceptional but feels extraordinary, ensuring a luxurious and comfortable wear.

THE INTRICATE DESIGN

Take a closer look at the collaboration.

The collection introduces exclusive color palettes that reflect Jerry Lorenzo's signature aesthetic – muted tones, earthy hues, and bold contrasts. The carefully curated color schemes add a touch of sophistication to classic sportswear, setting this collaboration apart from the ordinary. Embodying the essence of exclusivity, each piece in the Prada x Adidas Jerry Lorenzo collaboration is a limited edition collectible. The numbered releases make these items not only a fashion statement but also highly sought-after treasures for enthusiasts and collectors alike.

The collaboration seamlessly integrates co-branded detailing, featuring the iconic Prada logo alongside the timeless Adidas trefoil. This dual-branding approach showcases a mutual respect for each brand's heritage while highlighting the synergy between luxury and streetwear.
